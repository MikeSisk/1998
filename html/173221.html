Ozark Vista

Piney Creek Wilderness Area, Missouri

This view is from atop the Pineview lookout tower along the north edge of the wilderness looking south. One of eight widerness areas in the state of Missouri, this 8,142-acre area is located in southwestern Missouri's Barry and Stone Counties on the Cassville Ranger District of the Mark Twain National Forest.

The nearby city of Branson offers travelers to the region another sort of experience.
